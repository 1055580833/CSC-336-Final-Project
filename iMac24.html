M1 iMac 24-inch

The 2021 iMac 24-inch comes in seven fun colors, including green, purple, blue and pink. Retail 24-inch iMac prices start at $1,299 for the M1-equipped all-in-one-desktop with a 256GB SSD. Additional storage space and an 8-core GPU option are available, with Apple resellers accepting pre-orders for the latest iMac starting on April 30, 2021.
