11-inch iPad Pro

Apple announced the latest 11-inch iPad Pro on March 18, 2020 with new updates to its cameras and processors. Pricing on the 11-inch iPad Pro starts at $799 for 128GB of storage and can be configured up to 1TB. You can choose between Space Gray or Silver colors. Cellular options are available for $130 more.
